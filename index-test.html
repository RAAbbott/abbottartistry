Who is Abbott Artistry?


Hi, my name is Lizzy Abbott


I started Abbott Artistry in January of 2018. I had only been creating macrame art for about 4 or 5 months, but I loved to do it and was already dreaming of having my own business and being able to sell my work! So, I decided to quit my job and start doing macrame full-time. I have now been in business for almost a year, and it has been amazing so far! I love making new designs and custom orders, and am always looking for a challenge! I have done quite a few pieces for weddings and other events, including boho backdrops and cake stands. In addition to macrame, I also make other crafts such as baby teethers and plant decor. I would LOVE to make you a piece for your wedding or home space, check out my Etsy to have it shipped right to you! If you have a request for a custom order, contact me using the form at the bottom of the page or DM me on Instagram.
