What Can We Make?

We specialize in Macrame, and have made multiple pieces for various settings such as weddings, home and living areas, outdoor patios, and even for your vehicle! We LOVE custom orders, and we always make sure to add our special touch to whatever we make. Each piece is hand-made and can be customized to your liking. We also offer other cute crafts, including a completely customizable piece for your little ones.


Where Can You Buy Our Products?

You can buy our products HERE or by clicking the "Shop" button at the top of this page! You will go directly to our Etsy page where you will be able to search through our selection and order the macrame or craft of your choice! If you see a piece in our gallery that is not on our Etsy page, contact us with the details and we will make a custom piece especially for you!
