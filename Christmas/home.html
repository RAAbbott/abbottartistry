Who is Abbott Artistry?


Hi, my name is Lizzy Abbott


I started Abbott Artistry in January of 2018. I loved to make macrame and it was always a dream of mine to have my own business and be able to sell my work! So, I decided to quit my job and start doing macrame full-time. I have now been in business for almost a year, and it has been amazing so far! I enjoy making new designs and custom orders, and am always looking for a challenge! I have done quite a few pieces for weddings and other events, including boho backdrops and cake stands. In addition to macrame, I also make other crafts such as baby teethers and plant decor. I would LOVE to make you a piece for your wedding or home space, check out my Etsy to have it shipped right to you! If you have a request for a custom order, contact me using the form at the bottom of the page or DM me on Instagram.

FAQ




  • filter_dramaWhat Can We Make?

    We specialize in Macrame, and have made multiple pieces for various settings such as weddings, home and living areas, outdoor patios, and even for your vehicle! We LOVE custom orders, and we always make sure to add our special touch to whatever we make. Each piece is hand-made and can be customized to your liking. We also offer other cute crafts, including a completely customizable piece for your little ones.

  • filter_dramaWhere Can You Buy Our Products?

    You can buy our products HERE or by clicking the "Shop" button at the top of this page! You will go directly to our Etsy page where you will be able to search through our selection and order the macrame or craft of your choice! Some pieces in our gallery are not on our Etsy page as they are custom orders, if you see one you like just contact us with the details and we will make a custom piece especially for you!

  • filter_dramaHow Much Do We Charge For Shipping?

    Shipping varies depending on the product size and weight. Check out our Etsy for exact shipping prices on our featured products!

    For custom orders: We always try to get the best deal on shipping for you from USPS (FedEx for larger orders), and will give you an estimate of the shipping price at time of order!

  • filter_dramaHow Do Custom Orders Work?

    For custom orders, just contact us with the details on what you want and we'll get it done for you! Please include an estimate of the dimensions, as well as any pictures of macrame that are similar to what you want. We also love bringing you our best creative work, so if you're not quite sure what you want let us know what style you are going for and we'll craft something you love!

    You can contact us by sending a message, emailing us at info@abbottartistry.com, or by sending a DM on our Instagram
